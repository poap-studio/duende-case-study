The campaign: A 7-day Instagram quest

How we turned a fictional character's lost items into a viral engagement engine

Meet the Duende

A beloved fictional character with 370K followers who "lost" 3 essential items and needed his community's help to find them.

The 7-Day Journey: Each Item = Different Instagram Action

Find the Hat (Sombrero)

Day 1 Story Reply
The Story: "I lost my hat! If you see it, tell me the magic word..."
Action Required: Reply to Instagram Story with secret keyword
Reward: Bot asks for email → sends digital hat collectible + link to personal collection page

Find the Envelope (Sobre)

Day 3-4 Reel Comment
The Story: "My important letter is missing! Only the code can unlock it..."
Action Required: Comment on specific reel with secret code
Reward: Bot sends envelope collectible via DM + collection page updates

Find the Boots (Botas)

Day 5-7 Share Reel to Story
The Story: "I can't complete my journey without my magical boots..."
Action Required: Share specific reel to your Instagram Story
Reward: Bot auto-detects share → sends boots collectible + completes collection

The psychology behind the campaign

Understanding the behavioral triggers that drove unprecedented engagement

Progressive commitment

Each action more "costly" than the last. Users who complete harder actions are more committed to the brand.

Collection completion

FOMO drives completion. Seeing 2/3 items collected creates strong urge to "finish the set."

Native platform behavior

Uses Instagram's natural actions (reply, comment, share) instead of forcing users to external websites.

Narrative investment

Users become emotionally invested in helping the character complete his journey.
