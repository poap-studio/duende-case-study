---

## 🎯 **CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW**

### **Client:** duende.ba 
- **Followers:** 370,000 Instagram followers
- **Duration:** 7 days (February 24 - March 2, 2026)
- **Investment:** $10,000 campaign cost

### **Concept:** Narrative-driven multi-touchpoint journey
The duende (fictional character) lost 3 reliquias and needed followers' help to find them. Each reliquia required a different Instagram action.

### **3 Touchpoints:**
1. **SOMBRERO (Drop 17):** Respond to Instagram Story with keyword → bot asks for email → sends POAP
2. **SOBRE (Drop 18):** Comment on reel with secret code → bot sends reliquia via DM
3. **BOTAS (Drop 19):** Share specific reel to your Story → bot detects and sends reliquia

**User Journey:** Users could collect 1, 2, or all 3 reliquias. Each collection updated a personal page showing their duende "getting dressed" with the items.

## 🧮 **KEY CALCULATIONS PERFORMED**

### **Lead Quality Segmentation:**
Using CSV cross-analysis, I identified:
- **Tier 1 (1 touchpoint only):** 2,556 emails (58.1%)
- **Tier 2 (exactly 2 touchpoints):** 1,172 emails (26.6%)
- **Tier 3 (all 3 touchpoints):** 672 emails (15.3%)

### **Conversion Funnels:**
**Funnel 1 - Story (Sombrero):**
- 141,624 views → 15,741 interactions (11.1%) → 2,940 emails (2.1% view-to-email)

**Funnel 2 - Reel (Sobre):**
- 150,000 views → 9,253 comments (6.2%) → 2,968 emails (2.0% view-to-email)

**Funnel 3 - Reel (Botas):**
- 147,000 views → 2,164 shares (1.47%) → 1,008 emails (0.7% view-to-email)

### **Industry Benchmarks Research:**
- **Email capture rate standard:** 0.1-0.5% from social media
- **Engagement rate standard:** 1-3% for 100K+ accounts
- **Cost per email standard:** $3-8 industry average

### **Performance vs Industry:**
- **Email capture:** 2.1% vs 0.1-0.5% = **4-20x better**
- **Engagement rate:** 11.1% vs 1-3% = **+370% improvement**
- **Cost per email:** $2.27 vs $3-8 = **25-70% cheaper**
